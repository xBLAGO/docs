Так же плюсом является совместная интеграция с мессенджером Telegram, что улучшает и упрощает разработку сети.&#x20;
>

>

## Management

The functionality of the **BLAGO** network is controlled through the telegram bot [xBLAGObot](https://xblagobot.t.me). In it you can create needs to digitize assets, manage finances, participate in voting and get support.

## Network monitoring

Actions to track all operations are tracked through a web application inside the [xBLAGObot](https://xblagobot.t.me) telegram bot or on the **BLAGO** **scanner** [website](https://xblago.github.io/web/). Here, transactions of all users are broadcast, which are grouped and summarized into statistical data.



{% hint style="info" %}
These components of the **BLAGO** infrastructure provide users with the ability to participate in the network ecosystem, manage their assets, create and satisfy needs, and monitor and analyze network transactions to ensure transparency and efficiency.
{% endhint %}
